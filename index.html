Pig Game Rules

Rule #1:

Roll a dice that randomize between 1-6 if you do get a 1 your current score will be 0 and the next player will be the next to play and roll the dice.

Rule #2:

If you do want to hold your current score and dont take the risk you can press the hold button and add your accumulated score into your hold score and switch the active playing to the next other player.

Rule #3:

Who ever make to hold 100+ scores will be the winner of the pig game.
